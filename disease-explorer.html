VME 158 Disease Ecology — SIR/SIRS Simulator

The SIR model divides the population into Susceptible (S), Infected (I), and Recovered (R). Immunity is permanent (e.g., measles). The flow is one-way: S → I → R.

Transmission Mode
Density-dependent transmission: The force of infection λ = βI depends on the absolute number of infected individuals. Transmission rates increase with population density. These are the formulas we will be using in the lab exercises!
